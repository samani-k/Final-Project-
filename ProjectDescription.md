# CSCI 1300 Final Project

## Theme
### Mukbang City
The player begins as an aspiring mukbang content creator with limited resources and the help of their friend and manager, Mia. By exploring Mukbang City, creating content, upgrading equipment and restaurants, completing challenges, and competing against a rival creator, they must gain enough followers to earn a spot in the famous content creator house, "MukHouse", before applications close in 28 days.

## Bundle
Money is the bundle in this game. Money is being collected through content creation, and "donated" by buying equipment upgrades and Shady Agency tricks. The amount of money out of 500,000 is viewed throughout the game on the terminal interface. 

## Characters
This game contains 7 total characters.
1. Mia (Manager): Collects Rent and Manager fee from Player. Player can ask Mia for information on certain game features.
2. Clerk: Works at the Equipment Store. Player can discuss equipment with Clerk. 
3. Agent: Works at the Shady Creator Agency. Player can discuss shady social media advantanges with the Agent. Agent bids player farewell. 
#### Rivals grant player a Featured Mukbanger badge if player wins in the competition. 
4. CrunchiestSon: First rival(Raising Cane's). 
5. TungTungTungEathur: Second rival(In-N-Out). 
6. FolkValleyMukbangs: Third rival (El Pollo Loco).
7. Unc_eats: Fourth rival(Hot Pot).

## Locations
1. Apartment: Talk to Mia
2. Raising Cane's: Create content and compete
3. In-N-Out: Create Content and compete
4. El Pollo Loco: Create Content and compete
5. Hot Pot: Create Content and compete
6. Equipment Store: Upgrade inventory items
7. Shady Creator Agency: Purchase shady advantages

## Inventory System
1. Camera
2. Lighting
3. Microphone 
4. Badges

## Limited Resource
1. Money - Used to travel, purchase food, upgrade restaurants, buy equipment, pay rent, and purchase advantages from the Shady Creator Agency.
2. Followers - Required to unlock new restaurants, sponsorships, competitions, and ultimately qualify for MukHouse.

## Time 
There are 28 days to apply for the Mukbang content creator house.
Each day contains 3 actions.
The day ends when the player decides or after all 3 actions are used.

## Tradeoff Equivalent
The Shady Creator Agency is the JojaMart Tradeoff equivalent. Buying from the agency can change the ending if player is too reliant, and can affect competition outcomes.

## Extra Credit 
Featured Mukbanger Rival System:  
Restaurants can only feature one creator at a time. The player can compete in a competition that occurs only once at every restaurant against the Featured Mukbanger for the badge title of "Featured Mukbanger".

## Tradeoff System
Shady Creator Agency: Buy fake followers and bot rival accounts before competitons, but for a cost and consequence. 

## Mapping Style
- Map includes different restaurants for content creation, the apartment, the equipment store, and the Shady Agency.
- Style: Connected location map that changes when locations are unlocked. Has current location marked. 
 
## Win/Lose Condition 
Win:
- Reach the required follower count by the end of day 28.
Lose:
- Day 28 arrives before the player reaches followe requirement for the MukHouse.
AND/OR
- Player loses if they ever reach 100 or more drama points.
AND/OR
- Player loses if they run out of money
